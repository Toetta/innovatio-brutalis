Revopoint Range — 3D scanning

3D scanning for reverse engineering and fit/clearance reference. Used to recreate obsolete parts, improve geometry or generate CAD input for CNC machining or 3D printing.

Typical Use Cases

  • Obsolete spare parts
  • Fit and interface validation
  • Scan → CAD → new component
  • Digitisation of existing objects

Deliverables

  • Mesh: STL/OBJ (as needed)
  • CAD: STEP (for reverse engineering / modelling)
  • Documentation by agreement
