The problem

“Why does bookkeeping always feel like punishment?”

Because the industry makes money when you feel uncertain.

  • Export CSV → copy/paste → hope it came out right
  • Click through menus that feel designed in 2009
  • “Auto-posting” that happens to be wrong… and then it’s on you

FU-BookKeeping is the opposite: automation where it’s safe — and control where it matters.

Built for real life: multiple companies, multiple currencies, bank flows, and tax accounts. Designed so you can’t accidentally sabotage your bookkeeping.

How it works

1) Import → inbox (staging)

Import bank and tax accounts from CSV or Excel. Everything lands in an inbox first. Nothing is posted automatically.

Why? Because “imported the wrong file” should be a non-event.

2) Post line-by-line or in bulk — with control

Pick an account per line, or use rules/suggestions. Then post when you’re happy.

Provocative but true: If a system can mass-post without you understanding what’s happening, it’s not “smart”. It’s dangerous.

3) Multi-currency without headaches

Invoices in EUR, USD, GBP? No problem.

  • Post in base currency
  • Store transaction currency + exchange rate
  • Handle FX differences correctly (gain/loss accounts)

You see reality. The bookkeeping stays correct.

4) Multiple businesses in one instance

More than one company or project? Switch in a dropdown — everything stays separate:

  • Separate JSON data files
  • Separate backups
  • Zero risk of mixing data

In short: control, traceability, integrity.
