About

FU-Bookkeeping is a demo/debug (work-in-progress) to show the approach and collect feedback. Not intended for real bookkeeping.

  • Offline-first: data is stored locally
  • Export: SIE4 + backup.json
  • Focus: vouchers, chart of accounts (BAS-ish), invoices
